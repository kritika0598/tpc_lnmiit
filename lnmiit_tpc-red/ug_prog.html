UG Programmes

The Institute aims to establish a world class platform for creation, dissemintion, and application of knowledge in the fields of Information Technology through research, pedagogy, and consultation, as well as to become an effective catalyst for technological and societal development of the country through interactions with industries and academia.


Development of a curriculum is an evolving process but the present distribution of courses is under following headings

  • Compulsory Core Courses: Compulsory and common to all branches

  • HSS (mostly electives): Humanities Courses

  • Compulsory Program Courses: Courses compulsory to a particular branch

  • Science Electives: Science courses

  • Program Electives: Courses offered as elective in a particular branch

  • Open Electives: Courses offered as electives to all branches
